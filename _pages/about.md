# 👤 About Me {#about-me}
I am a first-year PhD student at the [VLAA](https://ucsc-vlaa.github.io/)@[UCSC](https://www.ucsc.edu/), supervised by [Prof. Yuyin Zhou](https://yuyinzhou.github.io/).
